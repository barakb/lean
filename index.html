The 7 types of waste

Transportation

When a company is built in functional silos, every feature have to transport a chain of teams, Whenever there is a bug, the feature transport again back.

Waiting

Naturally each feature is waiting for the next team to finish its current task before it peeked up multiple time in its lifetime.

Overproduction

When each feature have to be processed by every team, and the productivity of the teams is not even there will always be overproduction.

Defect

QA is only started once all development is done, it is not part of the process, tests plan are not written at design time, quality is not built as part of the process.

Inventory

Lots of features on the way, started but not delivered.

Motion

Whenever we are creating an ad hoc team we move people unnecessarily.

Excessive processing

Whenever team has its own agenda, there is a risk of feature that is not needed. The question that we need to ask is: Who is the client? does it really need that.

What we should do

Build multiple cross-functional teams

  • product
  • QA
  • dev ops
  • front end
  • back end

pros

  • The team is accountable as a team to the features it is given.
  • Many of the waste types are greatly reduce.
  • It is possible to have continues improvement as a team.
  • It is more fun, there is a partnership, every team member includes QA dev ops get sense of achievement when a task is done.
  • Professional development
  • Variance between the teams, option based development

cons

  • Leading the team is harder, team leader need to know more, to ask the right questions
  • Professional development
  • Variance between the teams
